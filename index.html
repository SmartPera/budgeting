📱 Install on iPhone / iPad
Follow these steps to add SmartPera to your home screen like a real app:
1
Open in Safari
Use the Safari browser (not Chrome, Facebook in-app browser, etc.)
2
Tap the Share button
The square with an up-arrow — usually at the bottom center or top-right.
3
Scroll down → "Add to Home Screen"
You'll see the SmartPera logo and name preview.
4
Tap "Add"
SmartPera is now on your home screen like a regular app!
